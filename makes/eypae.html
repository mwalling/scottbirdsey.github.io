EYP

EYP is one of the largest architecture firms in the country, and I have been developing the current incarnation of their website over the past two years. Original design and content strategy were carried out by an incredible collaboration of Bond Art + Science, webmeadow, Pixels and Pulp, and Ethan Marcotte. I took prototypes and built the site out in Drupal 8, and have continued to iterate on both the design and author experience.

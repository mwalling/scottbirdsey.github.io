I’m a web designer & developer

I’m a web designer and developer with a passion for building websites and app both big and small, and with people of all walks of life.

Want to know more? Well, I like to think about websites a bit like this.

  1. I like to plot a course for what exactly a website needs to do, and a plan for how it will do it.
  2. Armed with a plan, design & content – based around brand and use – naturally come forth.
  3. Finally, code-base development and authoring tools are produced, with an eye for performance.

Want to work together? Contact me.

I’m also a member of the bike racing community

My love for cycling began in 2015 as a 15-years-long smoker craving new adventure and happier lungs. Now I race road, cyclocross, and cross-country. I am Creative Director for the NYCROSS series, a five-event cyclocross series in the Northeast, and a boardmember of Capital Bicycle Racing Club. I am the race director for Troy, and a brand ambassador for KTM Bike Industries.

I like to think about bike racing almost as much as I like to do it. So, you can expect lots of writing here on the topic.
